Frequently Asked Questions

Is Plaid or Finicity better for open banking?

Plaid is better for consumer-facing apps and B2C FinTech (12,000 plus US financial institutions, fast integration). Finicity (Mastercard Open Banking) is better for mortgage lenders and credit decisioning where verified income and employment data are critical.

How do Plaid and Finicity pricing compare?

Plaid: $0.30 to $1.50 per connected account per month depending on products and volume. Finicity: custom enterprise contracts, typically $0.50 to $2.00 per verification event.

How many banks does Plaid connect to?

Plaid connects to 12,000 plus US financial institutions covering approximately 200 million US bank accounts. Finicity connects to 16,000 plus institutions but with variable data quality at smaller banks.

What is CFPB Rule 1033?

CFPB Rule 1033 (finalized October 2024) requires US financial institutions to provide consumers and authorized third parties with standardized API access to financial data. Compliance timeline: large banks by April 2026, smaller institutions by 2027 to 2030.
